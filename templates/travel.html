Destiny Travel
[ Aquarius ]

This fixed air sign is ruled by Uranus, the planet of questioning and innovation. It’s also the humanitarian of the zodiac, plagued by injustice and driven to change the world. (Notably, the earth is entering the Age of Aquarius now—a cycle that only happens once every 2,150 years and manifests as “a blending of old and new, of science and caring for people.”) White believes a knowledge-gathering trip to Greece, the cradle of Western civilization, would make an inspiring match for the water bearer. “It’s about experiencing the old temples and all this ancient wisdom but then adapting those teachings to who we are today,” she says. “Aquarius has to put its 21st-century twist on it to truly benefit people.”
